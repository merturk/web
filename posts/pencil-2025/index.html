HB Pencils

merturk included in Reading
 2025-08-24  121 words   One minute 
Contents

Recently, I’ve been trying out different pencils that I can easily find in Edinburgh. Unlike in Istanbul, you don’t really come across big stationery stores here. If you’re looking for something more specific, Amazon is pretty much the only option.

I think the most popular and easiest-to-find pencils in Edinburgh are the Staedtler series.

I’ve had a few of them and have been using them daily for at least five months now. Honestly, I’m surprised by the performance of the HB pencils from Staedtler. Typically, HB pencils aren’t my favorite because they’re harder to write with and not as bold as 2B pencils. But Staedtler does a good job—the writing feels comfortable, and the darkness is almost just right.
